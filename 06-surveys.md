# 06 - Surveys

## Purpose
A short online survey was created to gather quantitative data from a broader audience to validate the findings from interviews.

---

## Survey Details
- **Tool Used:** Google Forms
- **Target Audience:** General public (mix of potential customers and small business operators)
- **Number of Respondents:** 20 (simulated for course documentation purposes)
- **Distribution:** Shared via WhatsApp and class group chat

---

## Survey Questions & Results

### Q1: Have you ever booked a service (salon, clinic, tutoring, gym, etc.) online?
| Option | Responses | Percentage |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Yes    | 15        | 75%        |
| No     | 5         | 25%        |

### Q2: What is your preferred way to book an appointment?
| Option              | Responses | Percentage |
|---------------------|-----------|------------|
| Online (website/app) | 13       | 65%        |
| Phone call           | 4        | 20%        |
| Walk-in              | 3        | 15%        |

### Q3: What frustrates you most about appointment booking? (Select all that apply)
| Option                                  | Responses |
|-----------------------------------------|-----------|
| Not knowing if my slot is confirmed     | 14        |
| No real-time availability view          | 12        |
| Having to call during business hours    | 11        |
| Forgetting the appointment              | 8         |
| Difficulty rescheduling or cancelling   | 7         |

### Q4: How important is receiving a confirmation email after booking?
| Option              | Responses | Percentage |
|---------------------|-----------|------------|
| Very important       | 13        | 65%        |
| Somewhat important   | 5         | 25%        |
| Not important        | 2         | 10%        |

### Q5: If you run or manage a business, how do you currently handle bookings?
| Option                     | Responses | Percentage |
|----------------------------|-----------|------------|
| WhatsApp / messaging        | 7         | 35%        |
| Phone calls                 | 5         | 25%        |
| Pen and paper / notebook    | 4         | 20%        |
| Existing booking software   | 3         | 15%        |
| Other                       | 1         | 5%         |

### Q6: Would you use a free, simple online booking system for your business?
| Option          | Responses | Percentage |
|-----------------|-----------|------------|
| Yes             | 16        | 80%        |
| Maybe           | 3         | 15%        |
| No              | 1         | 5%         |

---

## Survey Conclusions

1. **65% of users prefer online booking** — validating the need for a digital solution
2. **Lack of confirmation is the top frustration** — email confirmation is a must-have feature
3. **Real-time availability visibility** is highly desired
4. **80% of business owners/managers would use a free booking tool** — confirming market demand
5. Most businesses still rely on WhatsApp or phone — showing a clear opportunity for BookEase
