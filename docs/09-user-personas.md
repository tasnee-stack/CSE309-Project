# 09 - User Personas

## What is a User Persona?
A user persona is a fictional but realistic profile of a typical user. It helps guide design and development decisions by keeping real human needs at the center.

---

## Persona 1: The Customer

**Name:** Tanvir Ahmed
**Age:** 24
**Occupation:** University Student
**Location:** Dhaka, Bangladesh
**Tech Comfort:** High (uses smartphone daily, comfortable with apps)

### Background
Tanvir is a third-year CS student who regularly books sessions with a private tutor and visits a local barbershop. He's busy and values convenience. He often tries to book after business hours when he has free time.

### Goals
- Book appointments quickly without calling anyone
- Know immediately if his booking is confirmed
- Be able to cancel if his schedule changes

### Frustrations
- Businesses that only accept bookings via phone during working hours
- Not getting confirmation and showing up to a missed or lost appointment
- Having to message back and forth to find a free time slot

### Preferred Channels
WhatsApp, web browsers, mobile

### Quote
> "I just want to pick a time, press confirm, and get on with my day."

## Persona 2: The Admin / Business Owner

**Name:** Farida Akter
**Age:** 38
**Occupation:** Owner of a small beauty salon
**Location:** Chittagong, Bangladesh
**Tech Comfort:** Medium (uses Facebook, WhatsApp; not comfortable with complex software)

### Background
Farida runs a 3-person salon and handles all booking herself through WhatsApp and phone. She's excellent at her craft but finds managing the schedule stressful. She sometimes double-books or forgets appointments when she's busy.

### Goals
- Stop losing clients due to scheduling errors
- See her full day's schedule clearly in one place
- Spend less time on the phone and more time with clients

### Frustrations
- Juggling WhatsApp messages, phone calls, and walk-ins simultaneously
- Clients who don't show up with no warning
- No easy way to see monthly trends or busiest days

### Preferred Channels
WhatsApp, simple web dashboard

### Quote
> "I just need something simple that keeps my schedule organised without me having to learn complicated software."
