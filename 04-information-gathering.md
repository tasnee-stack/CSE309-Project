# 04 - Information Gathering

## Purpose
Information gathering is the process of collecting data and insights needed to understand the problem space, user needs, and market context before designing the system.

---

## Methods Used

### 1. Market Research
- Reviewed existing booking platforms: Calendly, SimplyBook.me, Setmore
- Identified what features they offer and what small businesses commonly need
- Noted gaps: most platforms are too complex or expensive for very small businesses

### 2. Competitor Analysis

| Platform      | Strengths                           | Weaknesses                          |
|---------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Calendly      | Very clean UI, easy setup            | Not designed for multi-service businesses |
| Setmore       | Good for small businesses            | Free plan is very limited            |
| SimplyBook.me | Feature-rich                         | Steep learning curve, expensive      |
| **BookEase**  | Simple, free, custom-built           | New, limited features initially      |

### 3. Informal Interviews
Conducted short conversations with 2 potential users (see 05-interviews.md):
- A local salon owner who currently takes bookings via WhatsApp
- A university student who frequently books tutoring sessions

### 4. Online Survey
Distributed a short Google Form to collect quantitative data (see 06-surveys.md).

---

## Key Findings from Information Gathering

1. **76% of small business owners** said they spend over 1 hour/day managing bookings manually
2. **Most customers prefer** to book online rather than calling, especially outside business hours
3. **No-shows are a major concern** — businesses reported 20–30% no-show rates without reminders
4. **Simplicity is the top priority** — business owners don't want complex tools; they want something that "just works"
5. **Email confirmation** is expected by customers as a baseline feature

---

## Conclusions
The research confirmed a clear need for a lightweight, easy-to-use booking system. BookEase will focus on simplicity, reliability, and core functionality rather than feature bloat.
